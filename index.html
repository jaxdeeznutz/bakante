Revenue Model

How Bakanté makes money.

Three complementary revenue streams designed to scale as the platform grows.

💰
Commission per Booking
5–10% commission on every successful booking made through the platform. Aligns our success with the success of our users.
⭐
Featured Listings
Space owners pay to boost their listing visibility in search results and on the map — reaching more renters faster.
🤝
Partnerships
Strategic partnerships with property developers, logistics companies, and local government units to expand supply and usage.
